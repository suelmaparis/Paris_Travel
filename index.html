About Us

Paris Tour & Travel is a boutique agency specializing in tailor-made trips to Cape Verde, Tanzania (Zanzibar), Portugal and the Caribbean. We design itineraries that match your style and budget, whether you are traveling solo, with family, or celebrating a special moment.

From flights and accommodation to guided tours and private transfers, we handle every detail so you can enjoy a stress-free journey. Our team provides support in both Portuguese and English, ensuring clarity and peace of mind throughout your trip.
